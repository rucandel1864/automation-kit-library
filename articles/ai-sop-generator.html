How to Create SOPs With AI Without Inventing Process Steps

AI can help document repeatable work, but it should not invent process steps, policies, tools, or quality standards.

A safer SOP workflow starts with messy real-world inputs and keeps a human process owner in control.

A Safe SOP Workflow

  1. Choose one repeatable process.
  2. Collect rough notes, examples, checklists, or a walkthrough transcript.
  3. Ask AI to capture known process facts.
  4. Mark missing information as missing.
  5. Generate an SOP draft.
  6. Process owner reviews and edits.
  7. Test the SOP on a real task.
  8. Improve it after operator feedback.

ROI Formula

monthly_value = runs_per_month * minutes_saved / 60 * hourly_cost + rework_cost_avoided

If a process runs 40 times per month, saves 12 minutes per run, costs USD 50/hour, and avoids USD 300 of rework, the monthly value is USD 700.
