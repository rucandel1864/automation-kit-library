How to Turn Meeting Notes Into Action Items With AI

Meetings are expensive, but the hidden cost often comes after the call: unclear owners, forgotten follow-ups, vague decisions, and duplicated admin work.

AI can help with the first pass, as long as humans still approve commitments.

A Safe Meeting Notes Workflow

  1. Collect approved notes or a transcript.
  2. Ask AI to summarize the meeting.
  3. Extract decisions, risks, open questions, and action items.
  4. Mark missing owners or due dates as "needs assignment."
  5. Draft a follow-up email.
  6. Human approves final commitments.

What the Prompt Should Include

Ask for an executive summary, key discussion points, decisions made, action items, owners, due dates, evidence from notes, risks, open questions, and confidence.

The prompt should also say not to invent facts, owners, due dates, or commitments.

ROI Formula

monthly_savings = meetings_per_week * minutes_saved / 60 * hourly_cost * 4.33

If a team runs 15 meetings per week and saves 18 minutes per meeting at USD 60/hour, the workflow saves about USD 1,169/month.
