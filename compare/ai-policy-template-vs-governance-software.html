AI policy template vs governance software

Governance software can be useful for larger organizations. A policy template is usually the better first step when a small team simply needs clear AI usage rules this week.

At a glance

OptionBest forWatch out for
AI policy templateSmall teams, first rollout, clear rules, low budgetRequires internal review and ownership
Governance softwareLarger organizations, tool monitoring, approvals, audit requirementsHigher cost, setup work, internal process change

When a template is enough

Start with a template when the real need is behavioral clarity: what staff may use AI for, what they must not paste into tools, when a human must approve output, and who handles exceptions.

When software may be better

Governance software may be worth it when the organization needs centralized tool controls, audit logs, automated approvals, policy enforcement, vendor reviews, or cross-department reporting.

Recommended path

Use a template to define the rules first. If the policy later proves hard to enforce manually, those rules become useful requirements for choosing governance software.
