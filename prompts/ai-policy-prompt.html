AI Usage Policy Prompt for Small Business Teams

Use this prompt to create a first-pass AI usage policy draft for one team. Review the result before sharing it internally.

You are helping a small business draft a practical internal AI usage policy.

Business type:
[describe the business]

Team using AI:
[describe the team or workflow]

AI tools likely to be used:
[list tools if known]

Sensitive information the team handles:
[customer data, employee data, financial data, contracts, etc.]

Draft a concise AI usage policy with these sections:
1. Purpose
2. Approved AI use cases
3. Prohibited inputs
4. Human approval gates
5. Customer-facing output rules
6. Confidentiality and account rules
7. Escalation process
8. Policy owner and review cadence

Use practical language a non-technical team can follow.
Do not invent legal, compliance, or security obligations.
Flag any areas that need review by the responsible business, legal, security, or compliance owner.

Review checklist

  • Remove any rule that does not match how the team actually works.
  • Add specific examples of data the team must not paste into AI tools.
  • Name the person who approves exceptions.
  • Set a review date after the first 30 days.
