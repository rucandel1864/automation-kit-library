AI inbound lead response workflow

Respond quickly without turning every inquiry into a generic, over-eager sales message.

When this fits

  • Leads arrive through forms, email, referrals, or marketplace messages.
  • The first response is often delayed because someone has to interpret the request.
  • You need qualification notes before deciding whether to reply, route, or decline.

Workflow

  1. Paste the inquiry and any available context into the lead follow-up prompt.
  2. Score intent, urgency, fit, missing information, and next best response.
  3. Create a short reply draft and a handoff note for the owner.
  4. Review before sending and update the CRM or tracking sheet.
