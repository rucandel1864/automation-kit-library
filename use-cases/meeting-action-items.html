AI meeting action item workflow

Convert messy notes into decisions, owners, deadlines, open questions, and follow-up messages.

When this fits

  • Meetings produce useful discussion but weak follow-through.
  • Action items are scattered across notes, chat, and memory.
  • Clients or internal teams expect a concise recap after every call.

Workflow

  1. Paste notes, transcript excerpts, or bullet points into the meeting prompt.
  2. Ask AI to separate facts, decisions, action items, risks, and open questions.
  3. Review the output and correct owner or date assumptions.
  4. Send the follow-up draft and store the decision log.
