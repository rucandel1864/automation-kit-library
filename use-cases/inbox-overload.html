AI workflow for inbox overload

Use AI to classify, summarize, and route incoming messages while keeping final send decisions with a human.

When this fits

  • Customer, vendor, and internal messages all land in the same inbox.
  • Important follow-ups are buried behind low-value notifications.
  • Replies need to be fast, but the team cannot risk unsupervised auto-send behavior.

Workflow

  1. Paste one message or a batch of messages into the triage prompt.
  2. Classify each item by urgency, owner, workflow type, and next action.
  3. Generate a short summary plus a draft response only when a response is needed.
  4. Route the output to the right person or queue for approval.
